# Korean Style Soup #
09/24/21

Add:
- Chicken Broth
- Korean Soup Spicy Paste
- Sesame Oil
- Garlic
- Green Onions
- Shoyu (Soy sauce)
- 1 slice ginger
- Sugar
- Sake
- A pinch of chili powder
to a pot

Simmer for ~30 minutes

Add:
- Shabu Shabu meat
- Napa Cabbage
- Kimchi
- Fish balls

Simmer until cooked

Add Soft tofu!

Serve!

## To Convert to Rice Soup ##

Add Rice, bring back to boil, drizzle scrambled egg on top
